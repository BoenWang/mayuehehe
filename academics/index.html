My High School

The school I attend is Lynbrook High School in San Jose, California.The population consists of predominately Asian decent, and Lynbrook is one of the better off or wealthier schools. It has a beautiful campus, with modern classrooms and sports fields, as well as lots of trees and bushes. It has a wonderful supportive enviroment for students as they have an abundance of student run clubs and multiple school wide events. The school spirit is truly contagious. Lynbrook offers many different elective courses ranging from arts to business, as well as lots of honors and AP courses. Coupled with the caring teachers, this school is amazing.
